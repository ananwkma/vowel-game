How to Play

1
Tap a letter in YOUR WORD, then type a new letter — the word submits automatically.
2
Each step must be a valid English word. You can only change one letter at a time.
3
Work your way to the TARGET word in as few steps as possible. Green borders show letters already in the right place.
4
Made a mistake? Use Undo to take back your last step.
