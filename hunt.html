How to Play

1
Find 6 hidden words in the letter grid — 3 easy and 3 hard.
2
Drag across letters to select a word. All 6 words belong to the same mystery category.
3
Words run left-to-right or top-to-bottom only — no backwards or diagonal.
4
Find all 3 easy words to reveal the category.
5
Stuck? Tap the hint button to reveal the first letter of a word.
